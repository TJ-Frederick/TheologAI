This dictionary is extracted from the **Semantic Dictionary of Biblical Hebrew** (SDBH; © United Bible Societies 2000-2023) and contains the lexical analysis for each available entry, with definitions, glosses, all Scripture references, and lexical semantic domains. SDBH is an ongoing project and to date around 90% of all words found in the Old Testament are included. The remaining 10% will be added once they become available. 

This resource is available in the languages English, French, Spanish, and Chinese (both traditional and simplified scripts). Other languages will become available once the localization work has been completed.

The data can be downloaded in either XML or JSON.

# Special Encodings:

**{N:001}** is a reference to the first footnote. Please note that an entry may contain multiple references to a single footnote.

**{L:Ahasuerus<SDBH:אֲחַשְׁוֵרֹושׁ>}** is a hyperlink, linking the word **Ahasuerus** in the text to its corresponding entry in SDBH.

**{S:00200300100012}** is a an encoded Scripture reference, pointing to Exodus 3:1, word element 6

The key to the encoding is BBBCCCVVVSSWWW (Book, Chapter, Verse, Segment, Word)
Segments are irrelevant for the Hebrew text, so they can be ignored.
Words are counted using even numbers only.
Hebrew words that are written together are still counted as separate words (בְּרֵאשִׁית is Genesis 1:1 consists of two words)

**{A:NIV}** is a reference to a specific text version or translation
